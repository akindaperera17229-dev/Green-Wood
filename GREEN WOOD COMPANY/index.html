Featured Tour

10 Days of Culture, Nature, Adventure & Beach Relaxation

Day 01 – Arrival → Pidurangala Sunset → Sigiriya

Arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport. Rest at Airport Garden Hotel (Katunayake). Sunset hike at Pidurangala Rock. Overnight in Sigiriya.

Katunayake ➝ Pidurangala – 146 km / 3.5 hrs

Day 02 – Sigiriya → Pinnawala → Kandy

Sigiriya Lion Rock climb. Pinnawala Elephant watching + Lunch. Transfer to Kandy, Dinner & Overnight stay.

Sigiriya ➝ Pinnawala – 91 km / 2 hrs. Pinnawala ➝ Kandy – 41 km / 1 hrs.

Day 03 – Kandy → Nuwara Eliya → Ella

Temple of the Tooth, Royal Botanical Garden (2 hrs). Tea Factory, Ramboda Falls, Scenic train ride. Visit Nine Arch Bridge. Overnight in Ella.

Kandy ➝ Nanu Oya – 77 km / 2.5 hrs. Nanu Oya ➝ Ella (Train) – 60 km / 3 hrs

Day 04 – Ella Hiking & Relaxation

Full-day trekking & nature exploring in Ella. Overnight in Ella.

Day 05 – Ella → Udawalawa → Ratnapura

Udawalawe Safari & Elephant feeding. Coconut Land & Factory. Overnight in Ratnapura.

Ella ➝ Udawalawa – 90 km / 2.5 hrs. Udawalawe ➝ Ratnapura – 107 km / 3 hrs

Day 06 – Ratnapura → Hikkaduwa

Cinnamon Factory & Ambalangoda Mask Village. Overnight in Hikkaduwa.

138 km / 3.5 hrs

Day 07 – Hikkaduwa Exploration

Turtle Watching. Coral Reef Exploration. Overnight in Hikkaduwa.

Day 08 – Hikkaduwa Adventure

Whale Watching. Surfing & Beach Leisure. Overnight in Hikkaduwa.

Day 09 – Hikkaduwa Leisure Day

Village Experience & Beach Relaxation. Overnight in Hikkaduwa.

Day 10 – Hikkaduwa → Colombo Departure

Colombo City Tour (Lotus Tower, Shopping at Laksala). Farewell Dinner. Departure.

116 km / 2.5 hrs
